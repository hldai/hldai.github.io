Hongliang Dai (戴洪良)

I was a CS PhD student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Yangqiu Song. I now work at Tencent JARVIS Lab. My research focuses on the application of weak supervision in NLP, Fine-grained Entity Typing, and Entity Linking.
I received my Master's degree in computer science from Zhejiang University, and Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology.
